115. Corollary. -- It appears then that the relation of scholastic philosophy to theology is real, but insufficient to define the former. This philosophy should accordingly be studied from two distinct points of view: --

(1) In and for itself; and with this study the historian of philosophy is mainly concerned;

(2) In its relation to theology; a secondary study, though one that may not be neglected.
