Something of the sort is in Eth. Nic. I, 8: "All existing facts are in harmony with truth, but truth quickly shows itself out of harmony with falsehood." A false opinion on a complex issue is never wholly false: it must be borne out by some measure of facts; and these facts ultimately fall in with the true opinion, and the true opinion with them.
