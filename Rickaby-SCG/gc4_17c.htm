Of God and His Creatures

"Under the names of Spirit, Lord, and God, we have here mentioned the Holy Ghost, the Son, and the Father, Cf. 2 Cor. xiii. Graces, ministries, and operations, are all one and the same thing, the extra ordinary manifestations of divine indwelling which then followed upon baptism, all the work of one and the same God" (Notes on St Paul p. 93).
