Of God and His Creatures

The terminology is from Aristotle, Eth. Nic. III, 1113. What St Thomas here calls voluntas, and elsewhere intentio is the Aristotelian boulesis. Choice, electio, is proairesis. See my Ethics and Natural Law, pp. 31 sq.; Aquinas Ethicus I, 52 - 54.
