Of God and His Creatures

See further 1 Cor. viii: 1 Cor. x, 19-28: Rom. xiv: 1 Tim. iv, 3, 4. These passages are interesting to the moralist, as showing the incidental binding force of public sentiment, creating a new obligation. There are some remarks on the value of sentiment in ethics in Political and Moral Essays, pp. 279-281.
